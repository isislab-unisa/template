ISISLab FAST THESIS TEMPLATE

1. Upload the ZIP to Overleaf.
2. Main document: main.tex
3. Compiler: pdfLaTeX
4. Edit configuration.tex:
     master or bachelor
     italian or english
     research-writing guide enabled or disabled
     appendix enabled or disabled
     reproducibility checklist enabled or disabled
5. Edit metadata.tex with the real thesis information.
6. Read or disable the template-only writing chapter in:
     content/writing-guide/
7. Replace the example files in:
     content/appendix/
     content/reproducibility/

The appendix and reproducibility checklist are optional and bilingual.
Checklist answers use Yes / No / N/A (Sì / No / N/A) plus evidence or
a short justification. Remove non-applicable items or mark them N/A.

This project intentionally has no TikZ, PGFPlots, tcolorbox, minted,
Biber, shell escape, custom fonts, or custom compilation command.

BIBLIOGRAPHY: ACM-Reference-Format via standard BibTeX.
The ISISLab affiliation page is automatically available in English and Italian.

The research-writing chapter contains bilingual example pages adapted for a thesis from Simon Peyton Jones's Microsoft Research presentation. Disable it before final submission.
