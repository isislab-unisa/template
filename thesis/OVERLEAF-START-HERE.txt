ISISLab thesis template - Overleaf / pdfLaTeX

1. Upload the whole ZIP to Overleaf.
2. Main document: main.tex
3. Compiler: pdfLaTeX
4. Edit configuration.tex to select bachelor/master and english/italian.
5. Edit metadata.tex, then press Recompile.

The bibliography uses standard BibTeX and is handled automatically by Overleaf.
