ISISLab Unified Thesis Template - Overleaf pdfLaTeX Edition
================================================================

Single-source project:
- Bachelor or Master selection with \thesisdegree{...}
- English or Italian selection with \thesislanguage{...}
- automatically translated demo content and interface labels
- UniSA front cover and ISISLab affiliation page
- transparent ISISLab watermark and blue/gold design system
- bibliography, charts, code listings, diagrams, tables, and callout boxes
- compatible with Overleaf's default pdfLaTeX compiler
- standard BibTeX bibliography; no Biber, XeLaTeX, LuaLaTeX, shell escape, or custom build command

Compiled previews are available in demo-pdfs/.
See OVERLEAF-START-HERE.txt and README.md for instructions.
